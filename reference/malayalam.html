Odanthane ente fees tharega. Abishekgan dayshamthode irungepoyi. Veedam verumenne thirumanum edithirunnu. Ayyal puthiya varigal alojhichu kondo irukayirunnu. Korachi divisumungakal sheshum abibhasakkan veendum doctorinte clinicilethi. Ithavana oru uunoo vadee ayathani puli vanathi. Doctorode paranu "eniku onam thane kanaan petanilya". Koracha coravi eniku sokanpedatha pettiyilya. Athe konde fees thirichipettunu.
Abibhasakan punthichirichu kondu doctor kodatha cashi noki "Ithi thangal pranja thuga illalo enne doctorode paranju". Doctor nungalude karcha thirichikittirunnu. Odanthane ente fees tharega.
