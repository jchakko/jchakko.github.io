Buffalo Wings

Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Season the wings with salt, pepper, garlic powder. Get a baking pan and spray with nonstick oil. Bake for 45 minutes flipping half way. On the stove heat up less than 1/2 cup of buffalo sauce. Melt in 2 tablespoons of butter. Toss the wings inside. Wait a few minutes and serve.

Macaroni and Cheese

Cook macaroni according to instructions. Save some pasta water. Melt a tablespoon of butter and add some flour to create a roux. Add milk then add some cheese. Continue stirring. When the cheese finishes melting add more. Add some pasta water back if it needs more liquid. Finally add the macaroni back and mix it together.

Coconut Curry Chicken

Chop and season 1.5 pounds of chicken breast and season with salt, pepper, garam masala, curry powder, and chilli powder. Dice up onion, garlic, and a little ginger. Saute for a few minutes then add in garam masala, curry, powder, chilli powder. Continue saute for a few more minutes. Pour in less than a can of coconut milk, half of one of those small tomato paste cans. Mix it up. Add the chicken and slow cook for 5 hours.

Spiced Shrimp and Burst Tomato

Season a pound of shrimp with garam masala, curry powder, chilli powder, ginger, garlic powder, pepper, salt. Take an 8oz or so container of small tomatos and cut some of them in half. Turn on the heat and cook tomatos for 2 minutes. Add the shrimp and cook for an additional 2 minutes. Add a tbsp or so of butter. Finish with citrus juice.
